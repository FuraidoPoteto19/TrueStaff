JOB DETAILS

The IT Support Coordinator plays a key role in maintaining the reliability and efficiency of the organization's IT infrastructure. This position is responsible for providing technical assistance to users, diagnosing hardware and software issues, and ensuring smooth operation of computer systems and networks. The IT Support Coordinator collaborates with internal teams to resolve technical concerns, maintains documentation of support activities, and assists in implementing system improvements. The role requires strong problem-solving abilities, effective communication skills, and the capacity to manage multiple support requests in a fast-paced environment.

  • Coordinate IT support activities across multiple locations with contractors, vendors, and internal teams.
  • Analyze and resolve complex performance issues and system outages, escalating concerns to vendors when necessary.
  • Provide second-level and third-level technical support for system-related issues.
  • Manage relationships with third-party service providers and coordinate on-site technical visits.
  • Work with Mobile Device Management (MDM) systems and external vendors to maintain seamless IT service delivery.
  • Install and configure computer hardware, operating systems, and networking software.
  • Provide technical assistance for servers, desktops, laptops, printers, scanners, phones, and wireless devices.
  • Monitor system performance daily and proactively address potential incidents within the production environment.
  • Maintain inventory control and lifecycle asset management for end-user computing devices
  • Review system configurations and gain a functional understanding of internal applications and development processes.
  • Create and maintain documentation for IT procedures, troubleshooting guides, and incident resolutions.
  • Experience supporting a corporate or multi-site technology environment.
  • Experience working with IT ticketing systems.
  • Strong organizational and administrative skills with the ability to handle confidential information professionally.
  • High attention to detail and ability to work effectively under tight deadlines.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Excellent customer service skills and strong follow-through on technical issues.
  • Results-oriented with the ability to manage multiple priorities efficiently.
  • Willingness to learn new technologies and take on technical challenges.
  • Professional, courteous, and collaborative work attitude.
  • Post-secondary diploma, degree, or certificate in Information Technology or a related field.
  • Experience working with Mobile Device Management (MDM) technologies.
  • Knowledge of Microsoft technologies including Exchange, Office 365, Azure, and Microsoft Teams.
  • Strong troubleshooting skills for both mobile and desktop technologies.
  • Working knowledge of computer hardware, software, and network equipment.
  • At least 4+ years of experience providing IT support services.
  • Experience using Jira Service Management.
  • Professional certifications or familiarity with IT service management frameworks.
  • ITIL Foundation certification or familiarity with IT service management frameworks.
  • Regular business hours with occasional evening or weekend work required for maintenance or project implementation.
  • May involve remote work or support across multiple locations.
