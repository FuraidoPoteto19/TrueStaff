IT SUPPORT COORDINATOR SALESFORCE ANALYST

JOB DETAILS

The Salesforce Analyst is a central member of the Enterprise Systems Team, responsible for administering, configuring, and continuously improving EHN Canada’s Salesforce platform. Supporting about 150 users across Admissions, Marketing, Client Care, Business Development, and Alumni teams, this role translates business needs into scalable Salesforce solutions. The ideal candidate combines strong Salesforce expertise with excellent communication and problem-solving skills to improve user adoption, automate key processes, and drive operational excellence across the organization

  • Stakeholder engagement: Partner with Admissions, Marketing, Client Care, Business Development, and Alumni teams to understand operational needs, capture requirements, and translate them into scalable Salesforce solutions
  • Requirements gathering: Facilitate workshops, working sessions, and business reviews; document user stories, process flows, and functional specifications
  • Salesforce administration: Support the Salesforce org through user management, profiles, roles, permission sets, and security settings while maintaining org health and governance
  • Configuration & automation: Build and maintain custom objects, page layouts, validation rules, formula fields, and automation using Flow Builder and Process Builder
  • Data quality: Manage imports, exports, deduplication, and ongoing cleansing with tools like Data Loader and Data Import Wizard to ensure trusted data across the platform
  • Reporting & insights: Create and maintain reports, dashboards, and visualizations that provide business users and leaders with actionable performance metrics
  • Cloud support: Optimize Sales Cloud pipeline and Service Cloud case management while supporting Marketing Cloud campaigns and Data Cloud customer data processes
  • AI and Agentforce enablement: Identify opportunities for Einstein, Agentforce, and predictive analytics to improve productivity, automate workstreams, and enhance client outcomes
  • Troubleshooting & support: Serve as an escalation point for Salesforce issues, diagnose automation failures, debug data issues, and resolve user-reported problems across departments
  • Training & enablement: Develop and deliver user guides, training materials, and onboarding resources to improve adoption and promote Salesforce best practices
  • Governance & compliance: Enforce change management, release processes, documentation standards, and platform compliance with organizational policies and data privacy requirements


  • Experience working with Salesforce CRM in a business analysis or administrative capacity
  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to interpret data and provide actionable insights
  • Excellent problem-solving abilities and attention to detail
  • Effective communication skills for collaborating with technical and non-technical stakeholders
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and priorities in a fast-paced environment
  • Commitment to continuous learning and staying updated with Salesforce features and best practices
  • Customer-focused mindset with a drive to improve user experience and business outcomes
  • 3–5+ years of hands-on experience in Salesforce administration and configuration
  • Proven experience working with Sales Cloud and Service Cloud
  • Strong proficiency in Salesforce automation tools, including Flow Builder, validation rules, and formula fields
  • Experience designing and managing complex custom object architectures and data models
  • Demonstrated ability to gather business requirements and translate them into effective Salesforce solutions
  • Strong troubleshooting and root cause analysis capabilities
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to convey technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders
  • Proven ability to manage multiple priorities and projects in a remote, cross-functional environment
  • Experience with Salesforce Marketing Cloud or Sales Cloud
  • Experience with Salesforce Marketing Cloud and Salesforce Data Cloud
  • Familiarity with Agentforce, Einstein AI, or other Salesforce AI capabilities
  • Experience within healthcare, mental health, addiction treatment, or other regulated industries
  • Experience with data integration tools (e.g., MuleSoft, Salesforce Connect, or third-party iPaaS platforms)
  • Exposure to Apex, SOQL, or Lightning Web Components (development experience considered an asset)
  • Experience working in Agile or Scrum environments
  • A fully remote work environment
  • The opportunity to make a meaningful impact within Canada’s largest mental health and addiction treatment network
  • A collaborative and supportive Enterprise Systems Team that fosters a culture of continuous improvement
  • Ongoing professional development and growth opportunities
